Coastal Systems Research

Understanding the interaction between tides, waves, and coastal morphology

Research Focus

We study how tides, waves, and sea level shape coastal environments. Our research focuses on:

  • Past and future evolution of barrier islands
  • River delta dynamics and sediment transport
  • Coastal hazards and climate adaptation
  • Integrated field and numerical modeling approaches
