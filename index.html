About Me

I am benjamí Calvillo Melero, PhD student at the polytechnic university of catalonia. My research is focused on the study of the dynamics of the seimentary dynamics in the catalan continental shelf using free and accessible numerical modeling data for the whole world (CMEMS DATA). Moreover, I study the morphological changes of the Ebro delta by means of numerical modeling, analysis of in situ data and satellite imagery data.
